Nerf Tech Target Overhaul

One of my most memorable hobbies growing up was modifying Nerf blasters to shoot homemade darts at high velocities. Using Georgia Tech makerspaces, I took a thrifted Nerf gun and replaced every internal part with hand machined aluminum in order to upgrade the stock plunger tube spring to an extremely stiff one. I also included a brief firing test demonstrating the power of the blaster.
